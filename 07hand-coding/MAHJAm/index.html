About the Site

This website is designed to provoke users like you to explore different music genres and find new songs that excite you. Our goal is to give you a unique experience that will allow you to learn more about music and feel more connected as a whole.

We are always searching for more songs to add to our collection to make the experience better, and we couldn't do to that without you, our wonderful users.
